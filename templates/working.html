🧠 How the Model Predicts Scores

The prediction is based on patterns learned from student data. Each factor contributes differently.

📖 Reading Score

Strong influence — Students who score high in reading usually perform better in maths.

✍️ Writing Score

Strong influence — Writing reflects understanding and often correlates with overall academic performance.

📚 Test Preparation Course

Medium impact — Students who completed preparation courses tend to score higher.

🍽️ Lunch Type

Medium impact — Standard lunch may indicate better nutrition, which can influence performance.

🎓 Parental Education

Medium impact — Higher parental education often correlates with better academic support.

🌍 Race / Ethnicity

Low influence — Used for pattern recognition but not a strong determinant individually.

👤 Gender

Low influence — Minimal direct impact, but included as part of overall data patterns.
